About the Organisation

Poverty Overrated is a non-profit organisation built on the belief that poverty should never define a person's future or limit their potential.

Our Mission

To empower individuals through education, skills development, entrepreneurship and access to opportunities.

Our Vision

A world where people are recognised for their abilities and talents rather than their economic circumstances.
